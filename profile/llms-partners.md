# Griddo — Partners Program

> **Parent document:** [llms-v2.md](./llms-v2.md)
> **Last updated:** April 2026

Griddo partners with exceptional agencies experienced in higher education to lead digital transformation for universities worldwide.

## Model

- No partnership fees
- Pure revenue share model with territorial exclusivity
- Joint co-marketing activities

## Revenue Streams for Partners

1. Commission on software licenses sold
2. Direct billing to the university for implementation and support services
3. Long-term recurring revenue equivalent to 2–3 FTEs per year
4. Projected lifetime value of 5+ years per client

## Partner Impact

- Drastically reduced implementation timelines (6–12 months vs. typical 2–3 years)
- Significantly larger project sizes (10x to 20x average departmental project)
- Winner-takes-all effect consolidating scattered budgets into single ecosystem
- No custom backoffice development required
- Institution-wide engagement (all faculties, schools, departments under one platform)
- Recurring engagement equivalent to 2–3 full-time professionals year-round

## What Partners Get

### Market Intelligence
- Competitive benchmarks, market analysis, and university-specific reports
- Identify qualified prospects and open conversations with data

### Tools & Resources
- Structured onboarding and certification program
- Complete technical documentation
- Design resources that accelerate projects and ensure high-quality deliveries

### Business Support
- Lead generation support
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Training and ongoing support

## Partner Services to Universities

- Full project implementation support
- Design system customization
- Multi-site ecosystem setup
- CRM integration configuration
- Migration services from legacy platforms
- Team training and onboarding
